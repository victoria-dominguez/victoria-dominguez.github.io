I'm a storyteller with a love for local news and finding the larger issues in smaller stories.

Coming from a family of immigrants and veterans, I lived in various parts of America and wherever I went, I wanted to learn about how each place got to be where it is now.

Currently, I am based in North Carolina's Triangle region but am a native of central Florida and an alumnus of Florida State University. In my spare time, you can find me tending to my small village of houseplants and watching true crime.
